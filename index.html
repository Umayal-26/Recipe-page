Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipie has been in my family for generation and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced
Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 375 degree Fahrenheit (190 degree celsius)
  • In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  • Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
